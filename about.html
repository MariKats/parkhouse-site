Who We Are

Park House is a Greek brand of handcrafted leather products, designed and manufactured in Greece. Cool luxury and timeless minimal chic, reflected onto our fine leather products, give elegant pieces with a modern twist.

The Brand

Comfort design, ergonomic shape, lightweight, smart details make high quality accessories ideal for the everyday needs of an It girl, a career woman or a hardworking mom, regardless of age. An earth color palette captures the brand with hidden colorful surprises on details. It is about creating fashion items not for a season, but for a lifetime.

The Founders

Fedra Katsamba and Jasmine Perou, the designers and owners of the brand, have always had a special affair with fashion and design. Their initial wish, before setting up Park House, was to create the bags they would like to keep. Having strong foundations in fashion and design (Fedra is a fashion stylist and Jasmine is an architect), the two founders joined forces to create, in Greece, high quality accessories made of fine Italian leather that the every woman needs.
